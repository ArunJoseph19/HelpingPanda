FAQs

  • How is Helping Panda better than other crowd-funding websites?

    • We charge a 0% fee for your campaign. We believe our job is to put forth your idea to the world and hence gather the much needed attention towards it. Helping Panda is designed for all categories.

  • Will Helping Panda help me with future endeavours?

    • We see to it, that your campaign is amidst the public view. We help you gather the desired amount for the project. Once that is done, if you want to continue with us, we would be more than happy to have you.

  • Can an NGO also campaign under Helping Panda?

    • Yes, NGOs can host campaigns for specific causes.

  • How is a fundraiser verified by Helping Panda?

    • Before starting a fundraiser, all campaigns undergo a KYC verification, based on the submitted legal identity proofs and other supporting documents.
    A Helping Panda executive also reviews the information submitted and if needed, gets in touch with the campaigner for further discussion.

  • When does my crowdfunding campaign go live?

    • The crowdfunding will go live immediately after your documents have been verified. Keep an eye on your inbox for any mail from us, we will also notify you before your campaign goes live.

  • How do you review my crowdfunding campaign?

    • We verify your credentials (PAN number,and social graph) submitted along with fundraiser details, supporting documents (if any) and publicly available information (Website, Facebook, Twitter accounts). Post that, we also do a call if we have further questions.

  • Can donors get tax-exemption for this?

    • INR donations made by Indian taxpayers can avail tax benefit if you contribute to a campaign towards an NGO which has an 80G certificate. Look out for the Tax Benefits tag on the campaign page. Receipts are usually issued once the campaign ends.

  • Can I raise money for anything?

    • Yes, absolutely. We want to help as much as we can to those in immediate need.

  • What if I don't reach my goal?

    • We give you the option to cash out how much ever have collected after the end date of your campaign. If the flexible option has been chosen as the Funding Method.

  • How many campaigns can I run?

    • You can have upto 3 campaigns running at a given time, given that the purpose for each of them are different. This is applicable only in the Plus and Premium plans

Helping Panda aspires to collectively give back to society, by building a bridge to fulfill that goal
